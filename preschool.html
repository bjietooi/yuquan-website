Our Story

A Mother's Vision,
A Bilingual Mission

育泉的故事

Founded in 2005, Yuquan Preschool was inspired by a mother's vision for her child. With extensive experience in early childhood education and Chinese language teaching, our founder recognised a gap — the lack of preschools able to truly develop strong bilingual foundations.

She established Yuquan as a place where bilingualism is not just an add-on, but a core philosophy, thoughtfully and systematically nurtured from the earliest years.

FAQ

Common Questions

What ages do you accept?
+

We welcome children from 18 months to 6 years old. Our programmes are designed for each developmental stage — Playgroup, Nursery, and Kindergarten.

What makes Yuquan truly bilingual?
+

We have our own proprietary Chinese curriculum and structured teaching system. Children build strong foundations in both Chinese and English through immersive, daily bilingual exposure.

Are enrichment programmes included?
+

Yes! We offer table tennis, tea appreciation, melodica, Chinese painting, and calligraphy — all at no additional charge.

How can I visit the centre?
+

Simply fill in the enquiry form below or contact us directly. We'd love to show you around and introduce you to our learning environment.

What is your operating schedule?
+

We operate on weekdays with both half-day and full-day programmes. Contact us for detailed schedules and availability.
