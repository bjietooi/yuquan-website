FAQ

Common Questions

What ages are your tuition programmes for?
+

Our tuition programmes are designed for children aged 4 to 6 years old, covering K1, K2, and P1 preparation levels.

How is Yuquan different from other tuition centres?
+

We use our own proprietary Chinese curriculum developed over 20+ years, with a structured teaching system and small class sizes for personalised attention.

What is the class size?
+

We maintain small class sizes to ensure each child receives adequate attention and guidance. Contact us for specific details about current availability.

Can I try a class before enrolling?
+

Yes! We offer trial classes so you and your child can experience our teaching approach firsthand. Fill in the enquiry form to arrange a trial.

Are materials included in the fees?
+

Yes, all learning materials — including our proprietary worksheets, workbooks, and resources — are included. No hidden costs.
